--- title: Super Countdown Tracker permalink: /apps/supercountdowntracker/ ---

Add the dates/times of important events in the near or distant future, and Super Countdown Tracker will tell you how much time is left.

Features

  1. Super Countdown Tracker is *super simple*!
  2. Add and edit events
  3. Add tags to your events
  4. Sort & filter events by date and/or tag
  5. Add notes to your events
  6. Notifications inform you when your countdowns end
  7. Completed events are automatically archived
  8. Automatic, adaptive dark/light themes

Planned Features

  1. Edit date/time of completed events (un-archive/reset)
  2. Recurring countdowns
  3. Custom date/time formatting
  4. Attach a custom image to events
  5. Opt out of auto-archiving
